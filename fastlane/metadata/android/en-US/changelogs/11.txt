Areada 1.1.0 is a large cleanup and polish update.

- Cleaner Home screen with Books, Reading, and Bookmarks tabs.
- ZIP support for EPUB, PDF, TXT, and FB2 files inside .zip archives.
- Sectioned plain-TXT notes and reader-only Book Notes.
- Reader Orientation and reading ruler controls.
- Better sorting, filtering, empty states, and row metadata.
- Localization groundwork with language settings and draft Nepali resources.
- Improved TXT encoding and FB2/FBZ handling.
- Internal refactoring and more JVM tests for safer future updates.
